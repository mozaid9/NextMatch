# Future Roadmap

## Phase 1: Harden the MVP

- Replace mock payment with Stripe PaymentIntents or Checkout.
- Confirm paid joins from Stripe webhooks in Cloud Functions.
- Add refunds, cancellation windows and automatic cancellation if a match does not fill.
- Add better Firestore indexes and server-side validation.

## Phase 2: Match Quality

- Smart team and position balancing.
- Waitlist support.
- No-show penalties and reliability scoring.
- Player reviews, ratings and verification.
- Recurring weekly matches and private group matches.

## Phase 3: Discovery and Communication

- Google Maps venue discovery and distance filtering.
- Push notifications for join confirmations, reminders and cancellations.
- Match chat.
- Invite friends.

## Phase 4: Operations

- Venue partnerships and venue dashboard.
- Admin dashboard.
- Dispute handling.
- Revenue reporting and payout workflows.
